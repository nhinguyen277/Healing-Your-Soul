General

There will be days when busy life makes us mentally exhausted no matter who you are, a student, or a working person. Each age group will have its own pressures in life and sometimes we may wish that we could rest and isolate from the busy world out there. So why don't you try refreshing your barren soul with a scent that helps you awaken your inner self and relieve your psychology. Come with the scents and traditional methods of the ancient Indigenous to experience an aromatherapy that refreshes your soul.

Aromatherapy

Throughout history, Using the smoke of medicine plants is the method that many cultures did to support cleansing and healing.Especially, burning sage is an ancient indigenous tradition intended to remove negative energy out of their space prior to spiritual ceremonies. With its healing properties, the scent of sage leaves brings balance to the space, eliminates bacteria and helps improve your spirit, relieves psychological healing and purifies the mind.

Method

Before burning bucket leaves, prepare a clean space by tidying, dusting, and cleaning the room. Then, turn on a wordless music video with natural sounds such as running water, falling rain, stream sounds,... and open the window for ventilation, or turn on the air conditioner. Now, pick up a small bunch of dried sage leaves, and light one end so that the smoke of the sage leaves spreads throughout the room. The scent will eliminate negativity in your space and mind, kill bacteria and keep the air clean. This is a way to purify your soul after tiring days, let the scent and music bring you back to yourself, talk to your inner child, and soothe the hurt in your mind.

Conclusion

Regularly using sage leaves to bring the best mental health to you. If you feel your mental health has improved, please leave us a comment. Wishing you good health and a good quality life.
